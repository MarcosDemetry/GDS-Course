## Getting *everyone* behind G/SDS

## G/SDS is for *all*

Industry *&* Academia

* Shared **problems**
<ul style='font-size:x-large;' class='fragment current-visible'>
<li> Access to (/*baking* of) data</li>
<li> Similar questions in different contexts</li>
